Early Life and Education

Itake Guruji was born on 03 March 1947, to a family of farmers in Parali Vaijnath, Beed district, Maharashtra State (present-day Parali-V). He was the first of five children born to Ganpattapa Itake and Anusaya Itake. Itake's family belonged to the Lingayat Wani community, which is categorised as an Open Class by the Indian government.
Itake Guruji completed his primary and secondary education in parali vaijnath at vaidyanath school and he completed his degree of B.com at Yogeshwari College Ambajogai and In the year of 1972 hanuman vyayam prasaran mandal he got first rank in BPED and then he worked as a sports teacher.
